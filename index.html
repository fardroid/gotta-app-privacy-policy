Personal workout planner for Android

Gotta builds personalised home and gym workout plans based on your fitness goal, available equipment, and schedule — no subscription, no ads, no account required to get started.

Get it on Google Play

What the app does

🏋️

Personalised plans

Generates upper / lower body splits tailored to your goal (mass, endurance, weight loss) and the equipment you actually own.

📋

Step-by-step guidance

Every exercise includes a photo gallery and numbered technique instructions so you always know what to do.

📈

Workout history

Tracks completed sessions, sets, and skipped exercises so you can see your progress over time.

☁️

Cross-device backup

Optionally back up your settings and history to your Google Drive so you can restore them on a new device.

Why we request Google account access

Google Sign-In and Drive access are optional. The app works fully without signing in. You only need a Google account if you want to back up and restore your data across devices.

Google account email

Used only to identify your backup file in Drive and to display your account in the Settings screen. Never shared with third parties.

Google Drive — AppData folder

We request access to the app's private AppData folder in your Drive. This folder is invisible in the Drive UI and accessible only to Gotta. It stores a single backup file containing your workout preferences and history. We never access any other files in your Drive.

drive.appdata

What we do NOT access

Location, contacts, photos, messages, full Drive contents, or any other Google service.
